Why SatarkScan Exists

Because every Indian deserves a free, instant fraud check before their hard-earned money disappears.

The Inspiration

It started with a WhatsApp message.

"I just paid ₹200 to a scammer. The QR code pasted on the wall outside a shop had been swapped. Is there any way to get it back"
10:42 AM ✓✓

There was no way to get the money back. That moment stuck with me. I realised there was no simple tool for regular people to verify a QR code before they paid.

There was no app that worked in Hindi, Marathi, or Tamil. No app that worked when the internet was down. And certainly no app that actually respected user privacy.

So I built SatarkScan. Not as a company product, but as the tool I wished existed when my family needed it.

The Scale of the Problem

UPI fraud is a silent epidemic.

India's UPI ecosystem is incredible — but that scale has attracted a wave of scammers. In FY2024, the government recorded over 13 lakh fraud cases worth ₹1,087 crore. And that's only what got reported — 51% of victims never file a complaint at all.
