The Short Version

SatarkScan is a free tool to help you make more informed payment decisions. It is not a guarantee of fraud prevention. Use it as one layer of protection alongside your own judgement.

1. What SatarkScan Does

SatarkScan is a free Android application that analyses UPI QR codes on your device and produces a risk score based on pattern recognition. It uses a local fraud detection engine that checks signals such as VPA patterns, transaction amounts, keywords, and known scam identifiers. All analysis happens entirely on your device with no internet connection required.

2. What SatarkScan Does Not Do

It is important to understand the limitations of the app before relying on it:

  • SatarkScan is not a fraud guarantee. A LOW RISK score does not mean a payment is safe. It means the QR code did not match the patterns our engine knows about at the time of analysis.
  • SatarkScan cannot detect social engineering. If a scammer convinces you over a phone call to scan a QR code that appears clean, the app will not flag it. No offline QR scanner can. Always verify payments you did not initiate yourself.
  • SatarkScan is not a bank or financial institution. The app does not process payments, hold funds, or have any relationship with your bank account.
  • SatarkScan cannot recover lost money. If you are a victim of fraud, contact your bank and file a report on the National Cybercrime Reporting Portal at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.

3. Use at Your Own Discretion

SatarkScan provides risk indicators, not financial advice. The final decision to proceed with any payment is entirely yours. We strongly recommend that you:

  • Never make large payments based solely on an app's risk score.
  • Always verify the payee's identity through a separate, trusted channel before sending significant amounts.
  • Be suspicious of any QR code shared with you via WhatsApp, SMS, or phone — regardless of what any app says.
